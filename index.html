Company Overview

SOM Enterprises is a registered proprietorship firm owned by Shiv Kant Dubey, specializing in renewable energy solutions, electrical infrastructure, and civil construction across Uttar Pradesh and beyond.

Our Mission

Deliver sustainable energy projects with profitability and long-term vision. We provide end-to-end solar energy solutions including design, installation, commissioning, and maintenance. Our integrated approach encompasses electrical infrastructure, project management, distribution, and civil works, positioning us as a comprehensive partner for India's green transformation.

Competitive Landscape

Competition

Local EPC contractors and regional installers offer basic solar solutions but often lack integrated expertise. National players focus on large-scale projects, creating market nuances and significant opportunity for firms with strong local presence and multi-disciplinary capabilities.

Opportunity

Uttar Pradesh and nearby regions present substantial growth potential. Increasing preference for sustainable and cost-effective energy solutions drives market expansion. SOM Enterprises is strategically positioned to capture this emerging market segment.
